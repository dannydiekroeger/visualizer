Help

Intro

Welcome to the Audio Visualizer!

We have a multitude of visual effects that you are able to access and explore.

The menus will appear if you hover your mouse over the canvas

There are three main audio inputs. You can either choose an audio file from your desktop (Audio File: Choose File), choose from one of the existing audio files (URL Audio), or click the microphone button to take in audio from the background.

To play the sound, choose your input, click submit, and then click play.

For the playlist, select a file using the button on the left. It automatically adds to the playlist on the right. Click play to begin playing. Back and Next skip to the next and previous songs in the playlist

All effects are listed and ordered in the left sidebar. Once "Play Sound" is clicked, select an effect category as well as a specific effect in that category. It will begin playing.

To enter full screen mode, click the "Go Fullscreen" button in the top menu.
